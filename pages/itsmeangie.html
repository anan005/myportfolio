Why UX-Design?

I could say i have found the passion for solving the most commom problem there is: Bad User Experiences.

But one thing they have in common to make a good user experience; empathy for the user.

I've always enjoyed working with helping people, and I still value the oppurtiny to do it. That being addressed, i'm looking forward to work as a UX-Designer, where making interactive experience friendly, esteticly and accessible for everybody in all kinds of situation.
